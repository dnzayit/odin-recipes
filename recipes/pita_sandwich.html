Pita Sandwich

Description

Give your morning meal a Mediterranean makeover with this pita bread-based sandwich.

Ingredients

  • 1 pc pita bread
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 spoonful olives
  • 1 handful of spinach
  • 1 spoonful crumbled feta

Steps

  1. Warm a pita in a skillet, then cut it in half and open up one half for a pita pocket (save the other half for another meal).
  2. n the same skillet, scramble a couple eggs with crumbled feta, a couple spoonfuls of chopped olives, and a handful of spinach.
  3. Stuff the cheesy, briny scramble into the pocket.
